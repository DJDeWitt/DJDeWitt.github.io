Hey there, my name DJ. Nice to meet you!

I am a Consumer, Curator, and Creator of Media and Technology. I'm learning to code so that I can make things that empower individuals and make the world a kinder, more fair, more empathetic place.

Below, you'll find a few experiences and interests that provide a pretty good snapshot of who I am. At the bottom of the page or in the site navigation, I encourage you to also check out my Blog, About page, and Portfolio.

Finally, if you would like to get in contact, please do so via email or Facebook, Twitter, or LinkedIn.

A few experiences:

  • Over the last year, I worked as Content Services Specialist, and later Head of Service Delivery, for Tempesta Media, one of Chicago's most successful new tech companies.
  • Over the last decade, I've built up a very particular set of skills...designing independent video games!
  • In 2013, I graduated from Northwestern University with a double major in English Literature and International Studies. Now, I put my writing skills to good use ranting out prose in blog format - some of which you'll be able to find on this very site.

A few interests:

  1. I'm a huge media/news junkie: I'm deeply interested in education and storytelling, and I voraciously consume movies, music, games, books, and my Twitter feed (check out some of the people I follow for hot scoops).
  2. I'm an activist: I'm an incredibly privileged person who has had unbelievable opportunities. I want to use what I've been given to break down systemic barriers and empower those who are less fortunate than me.
  3. I'm passionate...and zen: Spend thirty seconds on my Facebook profile and you'll see I can get pretty heated about the issues I care about. That said, stepping back, relaxing, and having a good time is an essential part of who I am.
